About Us

Where Compassion Meets Action

Our Mission

At Paws & Claws Rescue, our mission is to provide compassionate care and a safe haven for animals in need. We are committed to promoting responsible pet ownership and advocating for the welfare of all animals. Our values include empathy, integrity, and community involvement, and we strive to make a positive impact through education, adoption services, and support for animal welfare initiatives.

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by a group of dedicated animal lovers, Paws & Claws Rescue began with a simple yet powerful goal: to rescue and rehabilitate abandoned and abused animals. Over the years, we have expanded our services to include adoption programs, educational outreach, and community events. Our journey has been marked by numerous milestones, including the rescue of over 2,000 animals and the establishment of our own shelter facility. Through the support of our volunteers, donors, and community partners, we continue to grow and evolve, always keeping the well-being of animals at the heart of everything we do.

Pawliday

Where Wagging Tails and Happy Hearts Meet

Pawliday promises exceptional on-site pet daycare, recreation, and day-boarding services specially curated for the furry member of your family. Our dedicated team of experts is here to make your pet feel safe and comfortable, just like they feel at home, while you venture out of home to cope with your hectic work schedule.
