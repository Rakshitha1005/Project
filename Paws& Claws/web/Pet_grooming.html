Our Grooming Services

At Paws & Claws, we offer comprehensive grooming services tailored to the needs of your pet. Our experienced groomers provide everything from basic baths to full grooming sessions, ensuring your pet looks and feels their best. We use high-quality products and gentle techniques to make grooming a pleasant experience for your furry friend.

Services Include:

  • Bathing and Drying
  • Haircuts and Trimming
  • Nail Clipping
  • Ear Cleaning
  • Skin and Coat Treatments

Our goal is to ensure that each grooming session is stress-free and enjoyable for your pet. Book an appointment with us today and give your pet the grooming they deserve!
