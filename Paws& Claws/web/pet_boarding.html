Our Service

Your Fury Friend is in Safe Hands

At Paws & Claws, your dog will receive the love, care, and attention they deserve. Whether you're gone for an evening or a few weeks, we ensure that your furry friend wants for nothing. Our expert team provides a stress-free, nurturing environment where your dog can relax and feel right at home. Join a community of passionate pet owners who trust us to give their pets the best care!

Our playroom floors are specially crafted for your dog’s comfort. Soft and gentle on their joints, easy to sanitize, and perfect for happy paws, our floors ensure your dog’s wellbeing during their stay. We disinfect all surfaces and equipment twice daily using pet-friendly, safe cleaning products.

Our spacious and safe playrooms are designed to encourage fun, exercise, and comfort. No chain link fences here! We use walls to separate playrooms, reducing noise, preventing airborne contagions, and creating a calm environment for our guests. This way, your dog enjoys a stress-free stay without visual distractions from other pups.
